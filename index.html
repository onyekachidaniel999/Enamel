Common Questions

Everything else you need to know.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major PPO plans and will submit claims on your behalf to maximize your benefits. If you're out of network or uninsured, we provide clear, itemized estimates before any treatment begins so there are never any surprise bills.

What if I have severe dental anxiety?

You're exactly who we built this studio for. We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) for light relaxation, and can prescribe oral sedatives for longer procedures if requested. Just let us know when booking, and we'll factor extra time into your visit.

How long will my first visit take?

Plan for about 60 minutes. We don't rush the intake conversation or the exam, ensuring we have plenty of time to review your X-rays and discuss your goals without anyone looking at the clock.
